Made by us, for you.

To pass the time and to stay connected during quarantine, my friends and I turned to knitting. We would pick a project to work on together, and then would on our porches and knit! However, after almost two years, we have run out of people to knit things for! This is where Knit By CC comes in - this platform allows us to continue to work on projects together (even though we are on different campuses) while sharing our love of knitting with the wider community.
