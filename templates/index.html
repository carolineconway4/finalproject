Meet the Founder

Caroline Conway

Hello! I'm Caroline, the founder of Knit By CC. Over the pandemic, my friends and I were searching for safe ways to spend the time, and we turned to knitting together on our porches. The act of working on a collective project brought a sense of community and comfort to us when we needed it most, and we would love to share this joy with others through Knit By CC.
